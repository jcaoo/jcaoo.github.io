Comic Strip

This comic strip was my first ever big project on Adobe Illustrator. My group and I came had to sketch out the scnario we were creatng. Our scenario was based off a scene from the show, "Orange Is The New Black". The scene was about guards in a prison that were given the opportunity to get a higher position. As the guards exited, the female guards realize they were getting paid less than the male guards. Our comic strip depicts modern day sexism in the workplace.
